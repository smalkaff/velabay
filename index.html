The Development

Urban Waterfront Living, Redefined

Vela Bay is a landmark new launch by SingHaiyi Group, positioned at one of Singapore's last waterfront sites along Bayshore Road. Inspired by the elegance of Portofino and Monaco, the development offers 515 homes across two iconic 31-storey towers — designed for those who demand space, sea, and serenity.
